Thank you for participating in our study today. We are interested in a very simple question, namely how people pass the time when they have nothing to do.

What you will be doing

  • In a few minutes, you will be asked to sit by yourself in your room (without getting up to walk around or falling asleep), in what we will call the "Free Time" Period, which will last from 10-15 minutes.
  • First, you will receive some instructions for the Free Time Period.
  • Second, you will be asked to follow those instructions for the 10-15 minute Free Time Period
  • Third, you will answer some questions on the computer.

Click 'Next' for further instructions.

Free Time Period Guidelines

During the entertainment period, we would like you to spend the time entertaining yourself with one or more of the activities below:

  • Watching a television show or a movie
  • Reading an enjoyable book or magazine
  • Working on a puzzle (e.g., a crossword or sudoku puzzle)
  • Looking at web pages (e.g., Facebook, Youtube)
  • Playing a videogame
  • Listening to music or the radio

That is, for the next 10-15 minutes, you should choose whatever activities from the list that you would find most enjoyable.

Feel free to switch from one activity to another if you want. You may also do more than one at once (e.g., listen to music while surfing the web). The goal is to find something enjoyable to do.

Remember, your goal should be to have a pleasant experience, as opposed to spending the time focusing on everyday activities or doing work.
